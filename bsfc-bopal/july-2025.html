The One Thing Every Parent Worries About On Day 1 😌
"Separation anxiety. Let's talk about it honestly — and what we do about it."
StaticIGFBNot Started
▶
Caption
The morning drop-off. The tight hug. The little lip quiver. And then — the walk back to the car alone. Separation anxiety is one of the most common and least-talked-about parts of starting school. Here's what we know: children who show separation anxiety are securely attached. That's a sign of healthy bonding. What we do at BSFC South Bopal: - A familiar teacher meets each child at the gate - Transition rituals — a wave, a handshake, a secret signal - The first 15 minutes are always sensory play — familiar, calming, absorbing - We update parents at pickup — always It usually takes 2 weeks. Some children, 2 days. Tag a parent who needed to hear this today. #SeparationAnxiety #PreschoolParents #SouthBopalParents #BSFCSouthBopal
