📅 Posting Schedule
Mon + Wed/Thu + Sat for main posts. Friday for weekly reel. Stories: Mon, Wed/Thu, Fri, Weekend. No admissions pressure — pure brand building.
⏰ Best Times
Carousels: 9–11 AM. Reels: 6–9 PM. Stories: 8 AM daily + 8 PM. Guru Purnima post: 7–8 AM auspicious window. Weekend stories: 10 AM.
📸 Teacher Shoot Plan
3 teachers over 3 weeks (W2, W3, W4). Each needs: portrait, classroom candids, 20-min interview recording, BTS prep shots. Schedule early morning or after school.
🎬 Reel Shoot Notes
W1: creative play. W2: teacher interview (tripod, good light, lapel mic). W3: BTS morning timelapse. W4: compile best clips from all 4 weeks.
💡 Content Pipeline
Use story Q&A boxes to collect parent quotes (Post 6, 8), child's firsts (Post 11) — turns audience engagement into next month's content automatically.
🚀 August Bridge
S16 + Post 12 tease August. Plan: Independence Day (Aug 15), new classroom theme reveal, teacher series continuation, and community event if possible.
