Monthly site shoot model
🎬

One site. One shoot. One month of video content.

Each month, SIS visits one Veracity site at the start of the month. That single visit captures: site B-roll (feeds the Scale & Proof site spotlight + statics/carousels/Stories all month), one People & Culture interview (driver or manager — rotates monthly), and incremental footage for the evergreen Brand Intro Film. No repeat shoots, no multi-location logistics. Month 1 site: Tumb — the flagship ICD, largest fleet and TEU volume on the network.

Week 0 — set-up (before first post)

LinkedIn — Content Admin access

SIS needs Content Admin rights on the Veracity LinkedIn company page before any posts go live. Confirm with Jaimin.

Instagram + Facebook — Editor rights

SIS needs Editor rights on both IG and FB. Pages should be linked via Meta Business Suite for simultaneous posting.

YouTube — channel setup + Editor access

Refresh or create the Veracity YouTube channel. Update banner, About section, and playlists. SIS needs Editor rights. Brand intro film to be pinned as channel trailer once uploaded.

Twitter / X — copy handover process

Client IT team handles posting. SIS to deliver copy-ready briefs (text + image/video asset) 48 hours before each scheduled post. All 6 X posts follow this workflow.

Resolve factual inconsistencies

Before any post goes live, confirm with Jaimin: HQ location, fleet size, SLA %, states covered, ports covered, and Singrauli volume figures. No unverified stats to be published.

Lock the visual system

Master template kit: LinkedIn post card (gold/charcoal), Reel lower-thirds, Story frames, YouTube thumbnail, Twitter photo card. One cohesive look across all channels.

Schedule the Tumb site shoot — Week 1, Day 1–2

Confirm site access, shoot date, and point of contact at Tumb. Single visit captures: B-roll, driver interview (consent confirmed in advance), and brand film footage. This shoot is the dependency for nearly every video post in the calendar — lock it first.

Confirm driver for Month 1 interview

Jaimin/site team to identify and confirm a driver at Tumb willing to be on camera. Get consent, name, and role confirmed before the shoot date so the crew doesn't arrive without a subject.

Build the All Services Overview icon set

Day 1 launch post (LinkedIn, IG/FB, Twitter/X, YouTube) is fully design-led — no footage required. SIS to design a clean 7-icon set covering every service before Week 1 begins, so this is ready independent of the Tumb shoot timeline.

Key notes

One site shoot per month — the core production model

Every video and most photography in a given month comes from a single site visit at month-start. Month 1 = Tumb. This single shoot must capture: B-roll, the one People & Culture interview (driver this month), and brand film footage. Lock the shoot date and subject before Week 1 begins.

Only ONE People & Culture video per month

Not two. The monthly shoot yields exactly one interview — driver or manager, alternating month to month. The "People of Veracity" static carousel series runs in parallel and does not require a shoot, filling the remaining People & Culture slots.

Twitter / X — Client IT SPOC

SIS delivers copy-ready briefs (text + asset) 48 hours ahead of each scheduled post. Client IT team handles actual publishing. All 6 Twitter / X posts follow this workflow.

YouTube long-form — built from the same shoot

The brand intro film (2–3 min) is not a separate production — it's cut from the same Tumb shoot footage. Script approved by Veracity before the shoot date. Target upload: Week 4, Wednesday.

Verify all stats before publishing

Factual inconsistencies exist between the pitch deck and the website. Confirm fleet size, SLA %, HQ location, state count, and Singrauli volumes with Jaimin before any stat appears in a post.

IG + FB — mirrored content

Clubbed as one entry in this calendar. Each entry counts as 1 IG post + 1 FB post = 2 published. Total 13 entries = 26 published across both platforms.

Client permissions — proof posts

All proof and case story posts run anonymized ("a leading manufacturing client") until Veracity clears specific client names for public use.

Site rotation for future months

14+ territories available — at 1 site per month, that's well over a year of fresh Site Spotlight content. Confirm Month 2's site and interview type (manager, per rotation) by the last week of Month 1 so the next shoot can be scheduled early.

CXO LinkedIn calendar

Separate document. Not included in these post counts. Jaimin's personal LinkedIn is managed on a separate cadence.

PR activity

Veracity to brief SIS with PR angle, target outlets, and timeline. Placeholder held in Week 4. Slot fills once brief is received.
