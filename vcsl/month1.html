Resolve factual inconsistencies

Before any post goes live, confirm with Jaimin: HQ location, fleet size, SLA %, states covered, ports covered, and Singrauli volume figures. No unverified stats to be published.

Lock the visual system

Master template kit: LinkedIn post card (gold/charcoal), Reel lower-thirds, Story frames, YouTube thumbnail, Twitter photo card. One cohesive look across all channels.

Confirm ICD & asset list

GPS-tracked fleet photos, ICD site operations footage (Tumb preferred — largest site), India territory map, control room/dashboard visuals. Required for Week 1 posts and the brand intro film.

Brand intro film — pre-production

Brief the video team on the 2–3 min brand intro film: Veracity overview, 1,500+ fleet, 14 territories, 30,000 containers/month, proprietary tech. No client names. Scripted VO. Schedule shoot in Week 1–2 for Week 3–4 upload.

YouTube long-form — pre-production starts Week 1

Verify all stats before publishing

Factual inconsistencies exist between the pitch deck and the website. Confirm fleet size, SLA %, HQ location, state count, and Singrauli volumes with Jaimin before any stat appears in a post.

IG + FB — mirrored content

Clubbed as one entry in this calendar. Each entry counts as 1 IG post + 1 FB post = 2 published. Total 13 entries = 26 published across both platforms.

Client permissions — proof posts

All proof and case story posts run anonymized ("a leading manufacturing client") until Veracity clears specific client names for public use.

CXO LinkedIn calendar

Separate document. Not included in these post counts. Jaimin's personal LinkedIn is managed on a separate cadence.

Visual assets are critical

Real photography and video of Veracity's fleet, ICD sites, drivers, and control room will dramatically outperform design-only posts. Prioritize getting site access for Tumb, Patli, or Singrauli in Week 1–2.

Firebase status sync
