Key notes across all 3 months

  • Technology is the new laneAlpit's tech angle is strategic — why the group built its own platforms, what problem each solves, what it means for enterprise clients. Not technical. Umesh owns the operational tech detail.
  • No courier franchise / origin storyExplicitly excluded per client direction. Alpit's credibility comes from what the group has built and where it is going — not where it started.
  • 2 articles per monthLinkedIn Articles are the highest-credibility format for an MD-level profile. Both Month 1 articles are distinct — one on the 15-year journey, one on technology solving enterprise problems. Month 2 continues with a tech deep-dive article.
  • Content spread is deliberateThe previous calendar had too much content in Month 1. This version spreads the strong ideas across 3 months so each post gets full attention rather than competing for it.
  • Coordination with Om and NehalAlpit's group-level content complements Om's new business content and Nehal's RCSL CEO content. Posts should not land on the same day where topics overlap.
