Week 0 — set-up (before first post)

LinkedIn — Content Admin access

SIS needs Content Admin rights on the RCSL LinkedIn company page before any posts go live. Confirm with client.

Instagram + Facebook — Editor rights

SIS needs Editor rights on both IG and FB. Pages should be linked (Meta Business Suite) for simultaneous posting.

YouTube — channel revival + Editor access

Existing but dormant channel. Refresh banner, About section, playlists. SIS needs Editor rights. Brand intro film to be pinned as channel trailer once uploaded.

Twitter / X — copy handover process

RCSL Client IT team handles posting. SIS to deliver copy-ready briefs (text + image/video asset) 48 hours before each scheduled post.

PR brief from RCSL

Receive the Month 1 PR activity brief. SIS to align on angle, target outlets, timeline, and approval flow before execution.

Lock the visual system

Master template kit: LinkedIn post card, Reel lower-thirds, Story frames, YouTube thumbnail, Twitter photo card. One cohesive look across all channels.

Confirm 3PL asset list

Fleet photos (HAZ vehicles, rail rack), operations footage from at least one logistics site, route maps. Required for Week 1 posts and the brand intro film shoot.

Brand intro film — pre-production

Brief the video team on the 2–3 min brand intro film: RCSL overview, 20,000+ deployed, 28 states, in-house tech, people on the ground. No client names. Scripted VO. Schedule shoot in Week 1–2 for Week 3–4 upload.
