02

Expected performance — relative ranges, not absolute figures

LinkedIn
Engagement rate vs current baseline
Baseline engagement is near-zero on sporadic posting. Consistent operational + capability content typically lifts engagement rate meaningfully within the first month, per the SIS/Quess/Denave benchmark pattern.
+40% to +70%
Instagram + Facebook
Reach growth on Reels vs static posts
Reels and short-form video consistently outperform static posts in early-stage accounts. RCSL’s current content is 100% static/festival posts — the format shift alone typically drives a step-change.
+60% to +120%
Twitter / X
Impressions per post (new presence)
No prior Twitter presence to benchmark against. Month 1 focuses on consistent posting to establish a measurable baseline by Month 2.
Establishing baseline
YouTube
Channel reactivation lift
Channel is existing but dormant. Two uploads will not drive scale, but reactivates the channel and tests Shorts-format response ahead of a fuller Month 2+ plan.
+20% to +50% vs dormant baseline
Overall (all channels)
Share of voice vs benchmarked competitors
RCSL starts from a small fraction of competitor presence (per the competitive audit). Month 1 is about narrowing that gap’s rate of change, not closing it.
Low double-digit % → building toward parity over 6–12 months
⚠️ These are directional, percentage-based ranges drawn from the competitive benchmark pattern (SIS, Quess, Denave, TeamLease) and general platform behaviour — not a guarantee. We deliberately avoid quoting absolute follower/view counts in Month 1 because RCSL has no meaningful baseline on Twitter and YouTube, and a hard number this early would not be defensible. Once a month of real data exists, projections move from percentage ranges to numbers.

Week 0 — foundation set-up (before first post)

LinkedIn company page overhaul

Full description (BSE-listed 540590, 20,000+ workforce deployed, 28 states, 15+ years), branded banner, complete services section, verified website link. Pin the reintroduction post once live.

Instagram + Facebook profile clean-up

Consistent logo, handle, bio and link across both. Build Instagram highlight covers: Operations · People · Certifications · Hiring. Mirror profile details on Facebook.

YouTube channel revival

Existing but dormant channel — refresh banner, About section, channel trailer slot, playlists (Operations / People / Capability). Audit and archive or update any outdated legacy videos.

Surface the certifications

Move ISO 9001:2015, ISO 27001:2013 and CMMi Level-3 from buried to featured — LinkedIn Featured section + a pinned credibility graphic.

Lock the visual system

Master template kit: data-card layout, carousel grid, Reel/Shorts lower-thirds, "People of RCSL" portrait frame, Twitter stat card, YouTube thumbnail template.

Confirm 3PL asset list with RCSL

Fleet photos (HAZ vehicles, rail rack), operations footage from at least one logistics site, route maps, and any measurable delivery outcomes for the anonymized proof posts.

PR brief from RCSL

Receive the Month 1 PR activity brief. SIS to align on angle, target outlets, timeline, and approval flow before execution.
