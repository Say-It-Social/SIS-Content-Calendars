Week 1 — Brand & Founder

Who is Sciro. Why it exists. Who built it. No product yet — pure brand and credibility. The first impression has to earn the follow.

Week 1MondayBrandReel · 30–45s★ First post

This is Sciro. No fillers. No shortcuts. Just performance nutrition done right.

Hook

The supplement industry has been lying to you. We built Sciro to fix that.

Content direction

Brand manifesto reel. Fast-paced, dark aesthetic, bold text overlays. Introduces Sciro as a no-BS performance brand — what it stands against (underdosed formulas, proprietary blends, marketing over science) and what it stands for. No product shown yet. First 3 seconds must stop the scroll.

Visual brief

Dark background, green (#D4FF4B) text accents, gym/lab footage or motion graphics, punchy cuts, bass-heavy sound.

Needs:Footage: gym shots or brand motion graphics. Music: cleared for IG use.

Key notes — launch month

  • Product shoot must be done before Week 3The entire teaser and reveal sequence from Tuesday Week 3 onwards depends on having high-quality product photography ready. This is the single most time-sensitive production dependency of the month.
  • Website live before launch dayThe early access CTA goes live in Week 3. The purchase link goes live on Week 4 Wednesday. Both need to be tested and working before the relevant posts go live — a broken link on launch day is unrecoverable.
  • All ingredient and dosing claims verifiedWeek 2 science content references clinical dosing. Every stat needs a credible source. Label claims need to match what's in the product exactly.
  • Launch day boost budgetWeek 4 Wednesday launch reel is the highest-priority post for paid amplification if budget allows. Even a small boost on launch day reaches a far larger relevant audience than organic alone.
  • Month 2 directionBuild on what launched. Introduce a second product or flavour, deepen science content, grow the athlete community, add testimonials and UGC as real customer feedback comes in.
